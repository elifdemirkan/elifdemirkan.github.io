WHO IS TAMER DEMİRKAN

HE CAME TO THE WORLD IN AKSARAY IN 1970. COMPLETED PRIMARY, MIDDLE AND HIGH SCHOOL EDUCATION IN AKSARAY. He started his higher education in 1986 at Akdeniz University, Isparta Eğirdir Fisheries Faculty, and completed it in 1990. In 1988, he did his aquaculture internship at Bağcı Aquaculture facilities, one of the leading companies in the sector, located in Köyceğiz Beyobası district. After this work experience that lasted for two years, he worked as a business manager in Antalya Kemer Taşlar Aquaculture in 1993 and then did his short-term military service. After returning from military service, he worked as a business manager at AKAR Aquaculture for nearly two years.

Between 1996-1998, he completed his master's degree in "Fish Diseases" at Süleyman Demirel University, Institute of Science. After nearly 10 years of experience in the sector, Tamer Demirkan established SU-TEK Aquaculture Limited Company, which provides services to the sector in subjects such as fish health, facility projects and production consultancy. br />
Tamer Demirkan established the first production and breeding facility on behalf of his own company in 2004 on the Özlen Stream in Karadere District. Then, in 2006, HAT Aquaculture established trout farming facility in Ören Çaygözü.
