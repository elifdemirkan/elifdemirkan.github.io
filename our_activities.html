TROUT BREEDING

Continuing its work by focusing on hygiene, quality management and maximum customer satisfaction with its years of experience and knowledge, HAT Aquaculture has an annual fish processing capacity of 1500 tons as of today, making significant contributions to both the regional economy and the employment of the region. Our quality trout, which we produce in our farms in Ören/Çaygözü, Karadere/Özlen Stream, Eskişehir/Çifteler and Manavgat/Gündoğmuş, which do not change the physical and chemical parameters of the water throughout the year due to the direct source of their water, are processed and packaged in our factories and delivered to the Turkish and world markets in accordance with the demands of our customers. We offer.

Fish harvested at the facilities can be delivered to our fish processing facility within a maximum of 30 minutes.

Our trout raised in our facilities are subject to a strict fish health control and the most important link of this is the vaccination program, keeping the fish health under control, and no antibiotics are used, from 20 g weight to portion weight.

It is regularly audited by our ministry. In terms of our environmental awareness, we leave the water we use to the stream bed only after we purify it. We also meet our energy needs with the environmentally friendly energy we obtain from the hydroelectric power plants at the exit of the facility or the GES power plants in the facilities where we breed. FISH PROCESSING FACILITY

We have established our HAT Aquaculture Fish Processing Facility, which is located very close to these facilities, in order to deliver the trout we produce in our facilities, which have extremely suitable aquaculture conditions, to the final consumer in a healthy way. Our fish processing facility is located in the very center of the Eşen Stream Basin, one of the most important trout farming basins in Turkey and the world. Our quality in the final product is indisputable since we are the fish processing facility closest to this basin with a trout farming capacity of 25 thousand tons/year.

The production capacity of this facility is 1500 tons/year. In our facility, we give importance to the employment of women labor force.
